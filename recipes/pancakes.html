Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a meaningful start to anyones morning. These fluffy saucers will enrich your day with only a few bites.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups buttermilk
  • ½ cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. Dry ingredients: Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Wet ingredients: Beat the buttermilk, milk, eggs, and melted butter together in a seperate bowl. Do not combine the wet and dry ingredients until right before you cook the pancakes.
  3. Making the pancakes: Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix with a wooden spoon or fork until the mixtures are just-blended. Scoop the batter onto a hot, oiled griddle and cook until the pancake is bubbly on the top. Flip with a spatula and cook until both sides are a golden brown.
